Brightleaf is a band based in Durham, North Carolina.

They take their name from the region's flue-cured, brightleaf tobacco. Discovered by an enslaved man named Stephen when the wood fire went out during curing, he stoaked the fire with charcoal, and the intense heat cured the tobacco quickly, turning it bright yellow.

They draw inspiration from Southern music of Stephen's time, before the lines were drawn that would come to define the genres known as Blues, Country, Folk, Gospel, and Rock and Roll.
